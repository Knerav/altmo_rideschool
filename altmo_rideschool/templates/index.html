What is ?

AltMo Ride School is a Certified Bicycle Training Program where participants undergo a training that prepares them to properly participate in Indian traffic as a cyclist. Its main mission is to enable you to confidently handle your bicycle in city roads. This program is to promote active mobility in Bangalore and get more cyclists on the roads. A completion certificate rounds up the accomplishment of the participant.

Why are we doing ?

Our goal is to create enthusiasm and confidence amongst people by teaching how to safely handle the bicycle.

  • Creating perspective - Educate the public on rights and responsibilities.
  • Beginning with the notion that children are the country's future and tomorrow's citizens will assist to develop that worldview.
  • Promote women's participation in society by introducing cycling to the economically underprivileged areas of the population.

How are we doing this?

  • Improve their cycling skills.
  • Increase the participants' knowledge of traffic rules.
  • Popularise the idea of cycling.
  • Improve their health by using non-motorized transport.
  • Have every participant trained by a trainer
