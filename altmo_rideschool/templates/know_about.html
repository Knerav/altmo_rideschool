What

AltMo Ride School is a certified bicycle training program.

It prepares participants for safe cycling in Indian traffic.

The mission is to boost active mobility and increase cyclists on city roads.

Participants receive a completion certificate.

How

Improves cycling skills and traffic rule knowledge.

Popularizes cycling as a mode of transport.

Enhances public health by promoting non-motorized travel.

Provides personalized training by certified instructors.

Why

To foster enthusiasm and confidence in cycling.

Educate on rights and responsibilities in traffic.

Cultivate a cycling culture, especially among children.

Encourage women's participation through outreach to underprivileged areas.
